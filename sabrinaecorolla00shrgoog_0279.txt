 

Omnipotenee.

Beneath thy all-directing nod

Both worlds and worms are equal, God.
Thy hand the comet's orbit drew,

And lighted yonder glowworm too,
Thou didst the dome of heaven build up,

And form yon snowdrop’s silver cup.
Bownma.
